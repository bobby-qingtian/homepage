A short history of moving

Hi, I’m Bobby Huang, a project finance professional currently living in China after spending 14 years abroad.

Before moving back to China, I lived in Brazil, the UAE, Canada, and Australia, where I completed my undergraduate education. My journey has given me the opportunity to experience life outside of my comfort zone, where each cultural experience and human connection taught me to be naturally curious about the world.

Below, you will find bits and pieces of who I am, both professionally and personally, including my passion for building community through my love of travel, music, and food. I hope you enjoy reading, and I am always happy to chat about anything and everything related to my work and hobbies!
